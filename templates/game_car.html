Guidelines for Playing !

  • If you are playing for the First Time, Click on Start Setup button.
  • Be ready in a convient position to play game.
  • Use Mouse to draw boxes and Press ENTER to confirm the particualr region to act as Switch for corresponding Action name shown
  • Once you draw boxes for all the switches you will be directed to same page.
  • Now Click on the Start Game button to start playing the Game !

Note

  • The Boxes you have drawn acts as switches and gets activated when you move your Hand in that area. So, Make sure you have Proper Lighting in your room while playing the game.
